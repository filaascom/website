## Revenue-Based Subscription Addendum

The permissions granted by the MIT License above are conditioned on the
following commercial terms. Use of the Software in any project whose monthly
recurring revenue (MRR) exceeds a tier cap requires an active paid subscription
at the corresponding tier or higher.

### Tiers

The current tiers, caps, and prices are published at
**https://filaas.com/#pricing** and govern this License. The tiers in effect at
the time of this writing are:

- **Free** — projects with MRR **up to $1,000 / month**. No subscription
  required. Covered by the MIT License above.
- **Pro** — projects with MRR **up to $10,000 / month**. Requires an active
  Pro subscription ($29 / month or $278 / year).
- **Studio** — projects with **no MRR cap**, plus white-label rights and
  unlimited projects. Requires an active Studio subscription ($79 / month or
  $758 / year).

"MRR" means the monthly recurring revenue, in U.S. dollars, generated by the
project, product, or service that incorporates the Software (the "Project").
Each Project is evaluated independently. Non-USD revenue is converted at the
prevailing market rate.

### Tier Compliance

1. You may use, modify, and distribute the Software under the MIT License so
   long as the Project's MRR remains at or below the cap of your current tier.
2. If a Project's MRR exceeds the cap of your current tier, you must, within
   **thirty (30) days** of crossing the cap, either (a) upgrade to a tier whose
   cap accommodates the Project's MRR, or (b) cease using the Software in that
   Project.
3. Each subscription covers Projects operated by the subscriber. A single
   subscription does not extend to third parties, clients, or separate legal
   entities, except as expressly permitted by the Studio tier.
4. The Free tier remains available indefinitely for Projects that stay below its
   cap. There is no obligation to subscribe while within the Free tier.

### Termination

Failure to maintain an active subscription required by this Addendum terminates
the MIT permissions for the Project in question until compliance is restored.
Compliance can be restored by activating an appropriate subscription or reducing
the Project's MRR below the relevant cap. Termination does not affect compliant
use by other parties or for other Projects.

### Updates

The tier names, caps, and prices may change over time and are authoritatively
defined at https://filaas.com/#pricing. Changes to tiers apply to new
subscriptions and renewals; existing active subscriptions retain the terms in
effect at the time of their most recent renewal until the next renewal.
